### Quick Reference

**Genre:** Immersive Sim / Stealth-Action / Tactical Shooter

**Core Loop:** Steal secrets → Sell to rival nations → Climb role ladder

**Key Inspirations:**
- GoldenEye 007 (facilities)
- Hitman (social stealth)
- Dishonored (gadgets)
- Metal Gear Solid (tactical espionage)
- Counter-Strike (gunfeel)
- Mirror's Edge (parkour)

**Three Nations:** Russia, United States, China

**Role Economy:** Spy ($$$$$) → Elite ($$$$) → Hacker ($$$) → Guard/Scientist ($$) → Janitor ($)
